Burger King Kids Club Commercial - The Simpsons is an advertisement made by Burger King in 1990 to advertise their new brand of The Simpsons' nature styled toys. The advert contained a screamer in it.

The advert starts with Bart Simpson saying, “Look out, nature dudes, we’re coooming!” with animals like owls, deer, and gophers looking at the camera and hiding. It would then cut to multiple toys of Simpsons characters like Homer, Marge, and Maggie. This is also accompanied by a narrator saying, “Burger King is taking Homer, Marge, and all the Simpsons camping, including you know who!” After the narrator says this, it would immediately cut to a close-up of a Bart toy, accompanied by a loud scream. After the screamer, it shows a herd of African buffalo running away, and a collection of all of the Burger King Simpsons toys, with the narrator saying, “Now you can get one Simpsons character camping scene in every Kids Club meal at Burger King!”
